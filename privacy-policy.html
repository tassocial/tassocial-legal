How long we keep your data.

We only keep your personal data for as long as we need it for legitimate business purposes and in accordance with applicable regulations. If you stop using our services, you can close your account and your profile will stop being visible to other members. Please note that we will automatically close your account if it has been inactive for two years.
After we close your account, we will delete your personal information as described below.
To protect the security of our members, we have implemented a security retention period of three months from account closure or one year from account ban. We retain your data during that period in case we need to investigate illegal or harmful behavior. Retention of information during retention periods for security purposes is based on our legitimate interest, as well as the interest of potential victims.
Once the information retention period has expired for security reasons, we delete your information and only retain limited information for the specific purposes listed below.
a) We retain limited data to comply with legal data retention obligations. Specifically, we store transaction data for 10 years, in accordance with tax and accounting legal requirements, credit card information during a potential transaction dispute, as well as "traffic data" / records for one year, in accordance with the legal obligations to store data. Also, for five years, we keep a record of the consent given to us by the members, as proof that we comply with the applicable regulations.
b) We keep limited information based on our legitimate interest: we keep customer support information, tracking data and non-precise location for five years of download/purchase, in order to make decisions about customer support, exercise our rights and be able to defend ourselves in in the event of a legal request; information about the existence of old accounts and subscriptions, which we delete three years after the last account is closed, in order to ensure good and accurate financial forecasting and reporting; data from the profile for one year, in the event of a potential dispute, for the purpose of establishing, realizing and defending legal claims; as well as data necessary to prevent members who have been denied access to open a new account, for as long as necessary to ensure the safety and vital interests of our members.
